# TaskBuddy - MVP Scope

# Purpose

Define what will be built in Version 1 (MVP) and what will be postponed for future releases.

The goal is to launch quickly, validate the product with real companies, and avoid unnecessary development.

---

# MVP Success Criteria

The MVP is successful when:

* Companies can onboard themselves
* Employees can mark attendance
* Employees can apply for leave
* Managers can approve/reject leave
* Admins can view reports
* AI assistant can answer basic workforce questions

# Excluded from MVP

These features will NOT be built in Version 1.

## Mobile App

Reason

Responsive website is sufficient.

---

## GPS Attendance

Reason

Adds complexity.

---

## QR Attendance

Reason

Not required initially.

---

## Face Recognition

Reason

Expensive and unnecessary.

---

## Biometric Integration

Reason

Future enterprise feature.

---

## Payroll

Reason

Different product category.

---

## Shift Management

Reason

Not needed for first customers.

---

## Advanced AI Analytics

Reason

Need more data volume first.
